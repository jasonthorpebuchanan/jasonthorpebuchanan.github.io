In September, Jason Thorpe Buchanan will join the faculty of the Hybrid Music Lab at the Hochschule für Musik Carl Maria von Webern as Artistic Associate/Lecturer in New Media and Digital Technologies for Music, teaching seminars and workshops in audiovisual/intermedia composition and network music/telematic performance with colleagues Stefan Prins, Artemi-Maria Gioti, Christoph Mann, and Franz Martin Olbrisch.
